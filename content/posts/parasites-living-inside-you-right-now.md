## The Top 5 Most Common Parasites

1. **Hookworms**: These microscopic worms can infect up to 1 in 5 people worldwide. They attach themselves to the lining of your intestines and feed on your blood, making you a ticking time bomb of parasites.
2. **Tapeworms**: With over 100 species, tapeworms are a parasitic powerhouse. They can grow up to 30 feet long and live off your digestive enzymes, leaving you feeling weak, tired, and perpetually hungover.
3. **Giardia**: This water-borne parasite can infect up to 3 in 10 people worldwide. It's a master of disguise, able to survive in both warm and cold environments, making it a prime suspect in cases of digestive issues.
4. **Whipworms**: These pesky parasites can infect up to 20% of people in developing countries. They live in the large intestine and feed on nutrients, leaving you feeling bloated, constipated, and utterly miserable.
5. **Roundworms**: With over 100 species, roundworms are a parasitic force to be reckoned with. They can grow up to 3 feet long and live off your organs, making them a serious health threat.

* In ancient Egypt, around 1550 BCE, the Ebers Papyrus described a parasite infection that was almost identical to modern-day Giardia.
